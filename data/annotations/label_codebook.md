# Label Codebook

## Level 1

No sustainability-related content.

## Level 2

Sustainability is mentioned aspirationally, but there is no enforceable requirement or evaluation mechanism.

## Level 3

The document requests sustainability-related certification, disclosure, questionnaire response, or supporting evidence.

## Level 4

Sustainability is part of evaluation and receives explicit score, weighting, or points.

## Level 5

Sustainability is mandatory, pass/fail, or a condition of award.

## Aggregation Rule

Default document-level rule:

```text
Final document level = highest applicable and enforceable sustainability requirement evidenced in the document.
```

## Notes For Annotators

- Label the full document first.
- Then capture the strongest evidence spans with exact page reference.
- Do not use unrelated procurement clauses as sustainability evidence.
- If confidence is low, record it explicitly.
